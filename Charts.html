We calculated the mean sentiment over each day and then compared this to the closing coin price for that same period. As you can see, there is a clear relationship between price and sentiment, however it is unclear whether the sentiment causes the price to change or whether price impacts sentiment.
A count of the sentiment over the period resulted in a large number of neutral for all three coins, this was expected, as neutral words are more frequent. Dogecoin recorded higher positive sentiment while Ethereum and Bitcoin appeared to have an equal amount of both positive and negative.
